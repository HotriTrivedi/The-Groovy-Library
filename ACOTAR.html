A Court Of Thorns And Roses

About the Book

"A Court of Thorns and Roses" is a captivating fantasy novel written by Sarah J. Maas. Set in a world divided between humans and faeries, the story follows Feyre Archeron, a young huntress, as she is drawn into a dangerous game of love, magic, and political intrigue. When Feyre kills a wolf in the woods, she unknowingly triggers a chain of events that leads her to the treacherous lands of the faeries. There, she must navigate a complex web of alliances and rivalries while uncovering dark secrets that threaten both human and faerie realms. Filled with enchanting romance, thrilling adventure, and intricate world-building, "A Court of Thorns and Roses" is a mesmerizing tale of courage, sacrifice, and destiny.

About the Author

Sarah J. Maas is a New York Times bestselling author known for her compelling fantasy novels. Born in New York City, Maas began writing at a young age and published her debut novel, "Throne of Glass," at just nineteen years old. Since then, she has captivated readers with her richly imagined worlds, complex characters, and immersive storytelling. Maas's works often feature strong heroines, intricate plots, and vividly depicted settings. With numerous awards and accolades to her name, including the Goodreads Choice Award for Best Young Adult Fantasy, Maas continues to enchant readers worldwide with her captivating tales of magic, adventure, and romance.
